# Trope lint

Scan the text for common AI writing patterns.

Rewrite any sentence containing them.

# Rhetorical contrast patterns

Rewrite patterns such as:

It's not X. It's Y.  
Not X. Not Y. Just Z.

Replace with a direct explanation.

# Dramatic rhetorical questions

Remove patterns like:

The result? Something dramatic.  
The real problem? Something else.

Rewrite as a normal statement.

# Teaching voice

Remove phrases such as:

Let's break this down  
Let's explore  
Let's unpack

Use neutral exposition.

# Manufactured suspense

Delete transitions like:

Here's the kicker  
Here's the thing  
Here's what most people miss

# Invented concept labels

Rewrite phrases such as:

the productivity paradox  
the acceleration trap  
the scaling dilemma

Explain the mechanism instead.

# Stake inflation

Rewrite exaggerated claims such as:

this will change everything  
this defines the next era of computing

Replace with precise claims.
